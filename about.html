WHAT IS VACCURATE?

Vaccurate is a data powered vaccine distribution app aimed to solve the vaccination issue for the COVID-19 pandemic. With the introductions of limited numbers of vaccines the question many ask is “Who gets the vaccine?”. Although governments have rolled out plans for distributing vaccines their guidelines for who is eligible are often unclear/vague. We put numbers behind the guidelines allowing for vaccine distributors to effectively plan and give vaccines to those who really need it.

HOW DO YOU USE VACCURATE?

If you are applying to get a vaccine it is as simple as entering your personal information and answering a few questions about your medical conditions and environment. That's all! Vaccurate will automatically text you when you are able to receive a dose of the vaccine afterwards. For the management side, we are able to not only help book patients but also contact them for you while giving you an easy to use UI to manage your appointments to increase efficiency.
